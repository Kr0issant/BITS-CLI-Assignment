### Question 1: Linux Environment Verification [4 Marks]

**Scenario:** You have joined a company as a Linux support engineer. Before accessing company resources, you must verify your Linux environment and user account details.

Perform the necessary Linux commands and prepare a report containing:

* Username and groups associated with your account.
* Current shell being used.
* Current working directory.
* List of files and directories in your workspace.
* Network connectivity verification result.

#### Expected Submission (Folder 1)

* `Environment_Report.txt`
* Screenshots of commands executed.
* Brief observations (3–5 lines) for each command.
